Horizon Vertical

It is a puzzle game with platforms. Your character can't jump, so it's up to you to arrange the platforms in the best way. Touch a plus button (red) to select the appropriate size platform from the menu that appears. When you think your character can cross to the other side of the screen, click the go button (cyan). Be careful! One fall, and you'll have to do it all over again.
